WHO

Needs Counselling?

Counselling is NOT for crazies, it never was.

Counselling is for ANYONE, for EVERYONE who may be bothered by a short term, temporary tough phase. Or for anyone dealing with a chronic, long-standing challenge that's taking a toll on them

HOW

Does it help?

Counselling provides confidential support.

This means that everything you discuss with the counsellor is private, between you and the counsellor. The exception is when a counsellor has a duty of care to report something that threatens your wellbeing or the wellbeing of others

WHY

Do You Need Counselling?

It can help with positive events such as choosing a direction in life or reaching your full potential. Perhraps you are feeling a 'bit stuck' and would value the viewpoint of a counsellor.

There may be things from your past you would like to tackle or there may be things in the present that you want to try and change.
